Hi!

I'm a writer living on the south coast of England with my wife and our beautiful dog.
I write personal essays on queerness, cycling, coffee, and occasionally my relationship to pop culture.
Those are usually published on my Medium profile.
I share my poetry and occasional visual art on Instagram.
My short-stories loosely fall under the genres of horror and modern gothic.

I also have a TinyLetter where I share my weekly thoughts and life updates, along with my latest book/podcast/movie recommendations.
Check it out and do subscribe!
I also have a Bookshop.org profile where I recommend my favourite reads of all genres.
